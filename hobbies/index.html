Computer Games

I play many computer games but here's some of the more frequent ones I usually play.


Warframe

Warframe is a free-to-play action role-playing third-person shooter multiplayer online game developed and published by Digital Extremes. I began playing warframe since early 2019 and have played for over 1700 hours on this title alone. One of the main reason I like warframe is the choices the game gives you. For example, you can complete a capture mission by going in stealthily, capturing the target and running away, or you can run and gun everything in your way throughout the entire mission.


osu!

osu! is a rhythm game primarily developed, published and created by Dean "peppy" Herbert. I play osu! mainly for relaxation and at the same time challenge my hand eye coordination, don't let its simplicity fool you, osu! is challenging at the higher levels.


Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang. Minecraft gives me the power to create almost any idea I can think of. It's a great platform to develop my creativity and at the same time play and explore it's vast worlds with friends.
